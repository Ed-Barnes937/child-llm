# Seed Idea

**Concept:** A child-safe LLM chat experience with age-appropriate guardrails.

**Core insight:** Existing LLM chat products (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) are designed for adults. Children are already using them unsupervised, with the risk of receiving answers not tailored for their age. There's no first-class experience built from the ground up for children, with parents in the loop.

**Vision:** A learning companion that encourages curiosity, creativity, and exploration — with parental controls that respect each family's values.

**Status:** Early ideation — exploring the big picture before scoping.
